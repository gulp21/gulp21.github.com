Charly in Madagascar

Charly in Madagascar is a round based tactic shooter. The goal is to kill all enemies while surviving with at least one character. You have an arsenal of weapons and tools to deal damage, destroy terrain, heal and many more.

Please make sure that you have Java 8 installed! Otherwise, the game won’t start.
